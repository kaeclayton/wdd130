Exciting Expeditions - White Water Rafting

Are you looking for an adventure? Look no further! Exciting Expeditions is here to help you have the adventure of a lifetime on the Colorado River and throughout the Grand Canyon. We have experiences for all levels from novice to expert. We have one day adventures all the way up to a three-week experience!

History

Exciting Expeditions began two generations ago with our current fearless leader's grandmother Silvia Fulano. She had a love of the Colorado River rivaled by perhaps only those who revered it an age ago. She worked hard to make rafting the river a possibility. Her son Bradford grew up on the river and as such his love of the region made him want to carry on the family business.

Bradford's son Isaak carries on the tradition of his father and grandmother. The zeal and reverance for the beauty of the river and the Grand Canyon carries on with him.
