Our Trips

Experience the beauty of the Colorado River's famous Horseshoe Bend. This easy half-day float down the river is a favorite for families looking for a day adventure.

Lees Ferry is an epic adventure. Spanning a good portion of the Grand Canyon this one is a bucket list item. Don't let the length of this one scare you away!

Whitmore Wash and Bar 10 Ranch trips are perfect for an adventurerer who hasn't had a chance to get their feet wet, so to speak, but have been wanting to for a while.

Pipe Creek is for the courageous traveler that isn't quite ready to commit to the whole Grand Canyon trip yet. Get the challenging rapids without the full length of the Lees Ferry trip.
