Our Team

Isaak Fulano

Isaak is our fearless leader carrying on the family business. Isaak grew up like his father practically living in the Grand Canyon, on the Colorado River. He loves showing others how to love the adventure that is white water rafting. Isaak is married to Samantha and they have three children who are also growing up to love the river. In his free time (enter laughter here), Isaak enjoys fishing and writing short stories for the local newspaper. Aside from living life to the fullest on the river, Isaak has an ambition to write a book series about adventures on the river.

Micaela Bernini

Micaela has been with Expeditions for the past 12 years. She has loved her experience helping others find the joy of rafting. In her spare time Micaela uses her creativity to in painting and sculpting scenes and such from the Grand Canyon. She has several artworks displayed at different places in and near the canyon visitors centers.

Caleb Rain

Caleb is the newest member of Exciting Expeditions. While he is new to us he is not new to river rafting. He has spent time on rivers since he was a child and he is well versed in how to handle the rapids. Caleb is a gamer in his spare time and enjoys a variety of different genres of games. Another hobby of Caleb's is music. He entertains on the longer trips with his musical prowess.

Abram Knight

Abram is our tenacious guide. He, like Isaak, grew up in the area and knows all the ins and outs of the legends and lore of the area making calm moments on the river a real learning experience. Abram is an ultra-marathon runner and has ambitions to travel Europe on foot one day. Also he is an excellent comedian.

Finnley Fraggle

Don't let her age fool you, Finnley is a fierce one in the rapids. While she may be the youngest of our crew, she is the one who is the most adventurous in the water and extremely capable of maneuvering the rafts with ease. Finnley loves nature. Her love of wildlife, bugs and lizards makes her our resident expert on the subjects.
